Forrest Gump

Director: Robert Zemeckis

Genre: Comedy-Drama / Romance

Year Released: 1994

Awards: 6 Oscars (incl. Best Picture & Best Actor)
Fun Fact: Many historic scenes use groundbreaking VFX compositing

Forrest Gump follows a kind-hearted man with a low IQ who unwittingly influences some of the most memorable moments in American history.
Through his unwavering love for Jenny and his simple wisdom—“life is like a box of chocolates”—the film emphasizes perseverance, innocence, and destiny over cynicism.
It blends humor and heart while reflecting on friendship, love, and the power of doing the right thing.
